Investment Thesis

Essential Infrastructure

Building the backbone of the data economy through next-generation fiber networks and decentralized energy systems. The future runs on information and power—we're investing in both.

The entire economy is becoming a data economy. But data without infrastructure is just noise, and infrastructure without power is just expensive dirt.

Every breakthrough in AI, every autonomous vehicle, every smart city initiative, every remote surgery, every precision agriculture system—they all depend on two things: ultra-reliable connectivity and abundant, resilient power. Yet most of America still operates on copper wires laid in the 1960s and a centralized grid designed before computers existed.

We're investing in the unglamorous systems that make the future possible. Next-generation fiber optic networks that deliver gigabit speeds to overlooked communities. Decentralized energy infrastructure including micro-nuclear reactors that provide clean, reliable power at the edge. The picks and shovels of the information age.

This isn't about building the cloud. It's about building what the cloud runs on. While others chase software unicorns, we're laying the fiber and building the power systems that every unicorn depends on.

The Micro-Nuclear Revolution

Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) and micro-reactors are changing the energy equation. Factory-built, inherently safe, and deployable at the point of need, they provide 24/7 carbon-free power for data centers, industrial facilities, and entire communities. We're investing in deployment infrastructure, regulatory navigation, and operational excellence to make distributed nuclear power a reality.

  • 1-20 MW micro-reactor installations
  • Data center co-location opportunities
  • Industrial facility power partnerships
  • Remote community energy independence
  • Grid resilience and backup power
