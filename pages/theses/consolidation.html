Investment Thesis

Regional Service Consolidation

Rolling up fragmented local service providers to create efficient, reliable, customer-centric regional champions powered by technology and operational excellence.

Thousands of local service businesses. Fragmented markets. Inconsistent quality. We see opportunity where others see chaos.

Every community depends on local service providers—HVAC repair, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, home services. These businesses keep our communities running, but most operate with outdated systems, inconsistent service quality, and limited growth potential. Customers suffer from unreliable scheduling, poor communication, and unpredictable pricing.

We consolidate these fragmented markets into regional service platforms that preserve local relationships while delivering enterprise-level efficiency. By applying modern technology, AI-powered operations, and world-class experience design, we transform mom-and-pop shops into customer service machines.

This isn't corporate takeover. It's local elevation. We keep the local brands, the local teams, and the community connections. We just make everything work better—for customers, employees, and owners who want to retire with dignity.

The Consolidation Playbook

Proven strategies for rolling up fragmented service markets.

Strategic Acquisition

Identify and acquire strong local operators with established customer bases, solid reputations, and owners ready for succession.

  • Target 3-5 initial platforms per region
  • Focus on $2-10M revenue businesses
  • Prioritize customer density and reputation
  • Seller-friendly terms with earnouts
  • Keep key employees and local brands

Technology Integration

Deploy modern operational platforms that unify scheduling, dispatch, customer communication, and service delivery across all locations.

  • Unified CRM and service management
  • AI-powered scheduling optimization
  • Mobile workforce management
  • Automated customer communications
  • Real-time performance dashboards

Experience Architecture

Redesign the entire customer journey from first contact to service completion, creating consistent, delightful experiences at every touchpoint.

  • Online booking and instant quotes
  • Proactive service reminders
  • Real-time technician tracking
  • Transparent pricing models
  • Satisfaction guarantees

Operational Excellence

Implement best practices across the platform while preserving local autonomy for customer relationships and market-specific needs.

  • Centralized dispatch and routing
  • Shared procurement and inventory
  • Standardized training programs
  • Performance management systems
  • Cross-selling opportunities
