Our Story

Mattis & Co was founded on a simple premise: the best investors are those who've built, operated, and scaled businesses themselves. We're not career financiers. We're operators who happen to invest.

After decades of building and running companies across technology, infrastructure, and services, we saw an opportunity to do private equity differently. To focus on businesses that matter. To work with founders and management teams as partners, not overlords.

Today, we split our time between investing in overlooked opportunities that rebuild communities and advising leaders through the complex transformations that define modern business. Two practices. One mission: Building value that lasts.
